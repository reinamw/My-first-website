My Goal

  1. Have a basic understanding of what programming is about.
  2. Familiarize myself with HTML, CSS and JavaScrip.
  3. Look for programs that allows self-pace learning.
  4. Start the learning curriculum and hopefully complete one section a day
  5. Supplement learning materials from videos or websites that explains the concept.
  6. Hopefully practice everyday and build onto my current understanding.
  7. Start a presence on Github and post small projects on my repository.
  8. Repeatly practice and continue to build on small projects and gradually build on larger projects.
  9. Hope to self study for about a year or a year and a half
  10. At that time period, start preparing for interview questions and job application process.
  11. Hopefully, land on a software engineering job!
